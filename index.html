About Us

Based in Naga City, Camarines Sur, our mobile bar started in 2020 — just as the world began celebrating life again after the pandemic. Since then, we’ve brought handcrafted cocktails to every kind of event — from weddings, birthdays, and debuts to any celebration you can imagine.

At DJ’s Mobile Cocktail Bar, we believe every occasion deserves great drinks and even greater memories. Our team serves with passion, creativity, and a touch of class — making every toast a moment to remember.
